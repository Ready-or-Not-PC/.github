## About Ready or Not

Ready or Not is a tactical first-person shooter that simulates realistic SWAT operations in high-risk environments. Players take command of an elite tactical unit, planning and executing missions such as hostage rescues, bomb threats, and high-risk arrests.

The game emphasizes strategy, communication, and precision. Every mission requires careful planning, use of specialized equipment, and coordination with team members to achieve objectives while minimizing risk.
